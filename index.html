About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student at Zhejiang University, supervised by Prof. Hailiang Zhao.
I received my Bachelor’s degree in June 2021, and my Master’s degree in June 2024 from East China Normal University.

My research interests primarily focus on:

  • Out-of-Distribution Detection
  • Federated Learning
  • Backdoor Attack
  • Trustworthy Intelligent Systems

If you are interested with my research, feel free to contact me by email: zwling@zju.edu.cn.
